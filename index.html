🌱 Common Crop Diseases You Should Know

Leaf Spot

🍅 Tomato, 🌾 Wheat

  • Brown/black circular spots
  • Yellowing halos on leaves
Prevention: Crop rotation, remove debris.
Medium Risk Learn More →

Powdery Mildew

🥒 Cucumber, 🍇 Grapes

  • White powdery spots on leaves
  • Stunted plant growth
Prevention: Provide good air circulation.
High Risk Learn More →

Rust Disease

🌾 Wheat

  • Rusty orange/brown pustules
  • Premature leaf drop
Prevention: Plant resistant varieties.
Medium Risk Learn More →

Bacterial Blight

🍚 Rice

  • Water-soaked leaf streaks
  • Leaves turn yellow/white
Prevention: Avoid excess nitrogen use.
High Risk Learn More →

Early Blight

🥔 Potato, 🍅 Tomato

  • Target-like concentric rings
  • Lower leaves affected first
Prevention: Mulch to prevent soil splash.
High Risk Learn More →

Downy Mildew

🍇 Grapes

  • Yellow oily spots on top
  • White fuzz on undersides
Prevention: Improve drainage, prune vines.
Medium Risk Learn More →
