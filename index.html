🗺️ Crop Disease Knowledge Map

Comprehensive diagnostic database for advanced crop protection

Disease Name Key Symptoms Primary Causes Treatment & Prevention
Early Blight Brown spots with concentric "bullseye" rings, yellowing leaves. Alternaria solani fungus, high humidity, soil splash. Apply copper fungicide; Mulch base; Rotate crops yearly.
Late Blight Water-soaked lesions, white fuzzy growth on undersides. Phytophthora infestans, cool and wet weather conditions. Destroy infected plants; Improve airflow; Use resistant seeds.
Septoria Spot Small circular spots with gray centers and dark borders. Septoria lycopersici fungus, overhead watering, infected debris. Avoid overhead irrigation; Clean tools; Prune lower leaves.
Rust Disease Orange/red dusty pustules on leaf undersides, premature drying. Puccinia graminis fungus, spore dispersal by wind/water. Dust with sulfur; Increase plant spacing; Early harvest.
Leaf Mold Pale green/yellow spots on top; Olive-green mold underneath. Passalora fulva fungus, poor ventilation in greenhouses. Reduce humidity (below 85%); Increase ventilation; Sanitize.
